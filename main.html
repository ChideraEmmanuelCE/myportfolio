Our History

Here's a potential history for Brewery Ilesa:

The Birth of Brewery Ilesa:

In the heart of Osun State, Nigeria, lies the ancient city of Ilesa, where tradition meets innovation. It was here, in 2010, that a group of passionate brewers came together to create Brewery Ilesa, a craft brewery that would soon become synonymous with quality , creativity, and community.

The Early Days

The founders, led by CEO and brewmaster, Adeyemi Olowookere, were determined to bring a taste of the world to Ilesa. With a deep understanding of traditional brewing methods and a passion for experimentation, they set out to craft beers that would appeal to both locals and visitors. After months of trial and error, Brewery Ilesa's first beer, "Opon Ifa," was born - a rich, malty lager that quickly gained popularity in the region.

Growth and Expansion

As the brewery grew, so did its reputation. Brewery Ilesa began to collaborate with local farmers to source high-quality ingredients, supporting the local economy and ensuring the freshness and uniqueness of their beers. The brewery's taproom, designed to resemble a traditional Nigerian courtyard, became a hub for beer enthusiasts and locals alike, hosting live music events, beer tastings, and brewing workshops.

Awards and Recognition

Brewery Ilesa's commitment to quality and innovation earned it numerous awards and accolades. Their "Eshu" IPA, brewed with local spices and herbs, was named "Best Beer in Nigeria" at the 2018 Nigerian Brewers' Awards. The brewery's "Osun" Pale Ale, inspired by the mythological goddess of the same name, became a favorite among locals and visitors.

Community Engagement

Brewery Ilesa is more than just a brewery - it's a community hub. The brewery partners with local businesses to host events and festivals, supports local charities, and provides brewing workshops for aspiring brewers. The brewery's commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility has earned it a reputation as a responsible corporate citizen.

Today and Beyond

Today, Brewery Ilesa is one of the most respected craft breweries in Nigeria, known for its creative beers, warm hospitality, and dedication to the local community. As the brewery continues to grow and innovate, it remains true to its roots, celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Ilesa and the passion of its people.
